# AI-Powered Scrap Management and Recycling Optimization System for Hindalco Industries
An AI-powered industrial scrap management system designed for Hindalco Industries to track, categorize, monitor, and optimize scrap recycling processes through intelligent analytics, inventory management, and predictive insights.

The Hindalco AI Scrap Management System is an intelligent industrial waste management platform developed to enhance scrap tracking, categorization, inventory control, recycling operations, and data-driven decision-making. The system leverages Artificial Intelligence and analytics to improve recycling efficiency, reduce material losses, optimize resource utilization, and support sustainable manufacturing practices in aluminium production environments.

Project Objective
To provide a centralized platform for managing industrial scrap efficiently, enabling real-time monitoring, automated reporting, recycling optimization, and AI-based prediction of future scrap generation trends.
Key Features
Scrap Entry & Tracking
Scrap Categorization & Classification
Real-Time Scrap Inventory Management
Recycling Process Monitoring
AI-Based Scrap Generation Prediction
Interactive Analytics Dashboard
Reports & Data Visualization
User Authentication & Role-Based Access
Sustainability & Waste Reduction Insights
